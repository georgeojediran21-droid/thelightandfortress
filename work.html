Functionalities

What the alliance will do

Practical work from immediate assistance to long-term public advocacy.

Four work areas

Humanitarian, Advocacy, Enlightenment, and Network building

These units define how the alliance will act in the community.

Humanitarian Assistance

We will invest in humanity through direct support services, public awareness, counsel, relief coordination, and collaboration with allies.

Legal Advocacy Support

A legal advocacy platform will help challenge injustice, protect human rights, and defend lives and properties in society.

Public Enlightenment

This is the heart of the system: research, education, intelligence gathering, diplomacy, campaigns, and strategic advocacy.

Network Liaison Unit

The alliance will liaise with partners and volunteers to organize events and strengthen the network.
